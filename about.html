About The Company

Chateau Grona Group is one of ten largest wine producers in Ukraine. Our specialization is creating high-quality PRIVATE LABEL Wines for Chinese market.

Our vineyards are located on the "grape latitude" – between 46 and 47 degrees – the same as the famous French wine regions of Bordeaux and Burgundy. This allows us to get abundant harvests of high quality.

We grow European varieties: Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Muscats as well as local and rare varieties.
