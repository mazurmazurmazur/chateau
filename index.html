Who are we?

The “AGRO-YUG” winery (a member of CHATEAU GRONA GROUP) originates from a winemaking colony founded in 1927 by the French fund “ICA” on the territory of Bessarabia – an area of modern Ukraine with more than a thousand years’ history of winemaking. For almost 90 years of its history, just all of Ukrainian Bessarabia, the winery survived many challenges, economic successes and downfalls. And through all these years our winery has been one of the best known and most respected wineries of Bessarabia.
In 1926 French winemakers travelled around almost all of Bessarabia looking for the best areas for viticulture. They chose a site near the town of Kaushan, which they named “the ideal plot”. The Fund built a village and a winery equipped with the best winemaking facilities available at that time. 52 hectares of vineyards with European varieties were laid down around the village. In just a few years the colony became one of the most prosperous villages in Bessarabia, and its wines were supplied all over Europe including France. In 1937 the area was annexed by the Soviet Union. After World War II the colonists never returned to the village.
