Private Label & Custom Wines

Custom wines At your request we can design a wine blend for your market and can make sure that you obtain quality and consistency. We could provide various options to customise wine for you, such as:

  • Colour and quality
  • Alcohol and sugar content
  • Firmness
  • Capacity (3 types)
  • Customization of taste (wine blend)
  • Label (basic design, improved label)
  • Branded cork
  • Branded cap
  • Cork (agglomerate / natural)
  • Box (transport / freight)
  • Type of bottle (standard / VIP).

Order Process

Private Label wine bottling You can easily order private label wines from us by following our 10-step scheme for Private Label.

  • Contact
    Contact us through our web-site or directly through the export manager.
  • Type
    Select the type and colour of wine.
  • Variety
    Select the wine variety. We have more than 20 varieties of wines.
  • Design
    We will design a label, a box and a cork for you. You will receive an example of labels that the company has produced previously, and based on these examples and your preferences we will create a new design for you. We can also use your logo.
  • Strength
    You choose the wine strength. We can make the strength of wine from 12 to 14% ABV.
  • Sample
    We will produce a sample according to your preferences and after your confirmation we will produce the whole batch.
  • Order
    You choose the point of delivery.
  • Prepayment
    You send us a 30% to 50% prepayment for the production.
  • Production
    The whole batch is produced and you have paid us the second part of the cost. The client can choose to be present at the bottling stage.
  • Shipping
    The goods are sent to you.
