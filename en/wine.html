Winery

Tarutinsky district, the location of “Chateau Grona”, is the largest grape-growing area in Ukraine. It produces almost as many grapes and wine as the entirety of Crimea! Grona Winery is the biggest in the Tarutino area and one of the largest in the Odessa region.

The plant was built in 1927 by the Jewish Fund ICA (France) and has been working for 87 years! In Soviet times “Grona” was the leading winery in the Tarutino district. The capacities of the plant are as follows: 8000 tons of grapes processed per season to create 5000 tons of wine.

The plant comprises an area of 4 hectares, 1800 sq.m of production, 5 lines for receiving and processing grapestwo mini-power stations, a borehole, its own boiler facility and a licensed alcohol cellar. The capacity of bottling lines is as follows: nominal – 6000 bottles per hour, effective – 3500 bottles per hour.
