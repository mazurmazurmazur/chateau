Private Label in China

Private Label Wines allows to introduce a “one in a million wine” that no one else has. Today each big store, retailer or online store has its own private label. If such wines have premium design then the business-model will be more successful.

Chinese consumers prefer foreign wines, especially if those are created for special occasions or as gifts. As of today foreign import makes up 70-80% of Chinese wine market.
