Private Label in China

Why are Private Label wines so popular in China?

A consumer’s opportunity to buy a truly unique wine under original label is a fundamental driver of the Chinese wine market.

This task is being fulfilled by (Private label wines), allowing to introduce a “one in a million wine” that no one else has. Today each big store, retailer or online store has its own private label. If such wines have premium design then the business-model will be more successful.

Chinese consumers prefer foreign wines, especially if those are created for special occasions or as gifts. As of today foreign import makes up 70-80% of Chinese wine market.

Nowadays consumers’ attention has shifted to new wine regions: Australia, Eastern Europe (Ukraine, Hungary, Moldova) and South Africa.

Importers and distributors become not only buyers, but co-authors of wines starting from the design of the label to finishing off the peculiarities of the taste. It may be profitable for you to produce wines after private label, if you are:

  1. An importer or a distributor wanting to increase your prestige and profit by means of selling a unique product.
  2. A big retailer that has a positive image with consumers and produces a wide range of products under its own brand.
  3. A chain of cafes, restaurants or night clubs aiming at confirming its high status.

Additional advantage: if you sell wine under private label, buyers cannot find its prices abroad (for example, using www.wine-searcher.com), which allows you to maximize profit.
