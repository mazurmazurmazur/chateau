Why Ukrainian Wines?

Why are Ukrainian wines ideal for Private Label wine production?

Ukraine is a European country situated at the same parallel as the famous winemaking regions of Bordeaux and Burgundy. Winemaking in Ukraine has a millennial history. Ukrainian universities prepare highly qualified winemaking specialists. Cost of labour in Ukraine is considerably less than, for instance, in France. These factors would allow to obtain wine of good quality for a lower price.

Traditionally there are plenty of red wines in Ukraine produced both of classic and local grape varieties. Their multitude allows to satisfy the most exquisite taste.

Growth of Eastern European wines’ sales in China made up more than 500% in 2018 compared to 2017, which is the evidence of big potential of Ukrainian wines. At the same time import of wines from Western Europe significantly decreases.
